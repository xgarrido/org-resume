Curiculum Vitae

I’m an associate professor at the University of Paris-South, doing my research application at the Linear Accelerator Laboratory.

I’m interested in neutrino physics, looking for Majorana neutrino and thus searching for neutrinoless double-beta decay. I also contribute to and maintain some open source projects on GitHub.
